Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is one of the most delicious dishes in the entire world. With the right peices, right amount of spices, this is one dish that can take you out of this world.This is a simple dish that can make your meal a different level.

This is going to be one of the most fire dishes you will have ever eaten trust

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs, 2lbs, skin on, quartered
  • Lemon, juice from 1
  • White Vinegar, 1/4 cup
  • Coconut Milk, thick, 1 cup
  • Golden Potatoes, 3, peeled, quartered
  • Salt, 1-2 tbsp
  • Meat Masala (roast all the ingredients below on a pan until aroma comes out then grind into a fine powder; this roughly makes 1/4 cup) Cinnamon Stick, 1 Fennel Seeds, 2 tbsp Green Cardamom, 10 pods Cloves, 6
  • Green Chilies, 2, split with stem off
  • Ginger Chilies, 2, spilt witht the stem off
  • Ginger, 2 inches, minced
  • Garlic Cloves, 6, minced
  • Curry Leaves, 1 sprig (or 12-16 leaves)
  • White Onion, 6, thinly sliced
  • Ground Turmeric, 1 tsp
  • Ground Corriander, 2 tbsp
  • Red Chili Powder, 2 tbsp
  • Coconut Oil, 2 tbsp
  • Shallots, 2, thinly sliced
  • Dried Red Chilis, 2, broken in half
  • Mustard Seeds, 2 tsp
  • Curry Leaves,1 sprig (or 12-16 leaves)

Directions

  1. Clean the Chicken
  2. Marinate the chicken (using the 1/2 of the meat masala and all the ingredients listed till Red chili powerder).
  3. Cook the chicken and prep potatoes.
  4. Season it and mix it together
